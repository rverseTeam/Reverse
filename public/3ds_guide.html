foxverse Setup Guide [3DS]

Before starting this make sure you have the latest version of Luma3DS custom firmware!

Step 1: Download fv-setup.zip and save it on your computer.

Step 2: Extract fv-setup.zip somewhere and insert your SD card into your computer.

Step 3: Open the fv-setup folder where you extracted it and copy [olv, luma] to the root of your SD card.

Step 4: Insert your SD card back into your 3DS and hold [SELECT] and power on your console.

Step 5: Make sure "Enable game patching" is enabled, if it isn't then enable it.

Step 6: Press [START] and launch Miiverse!


That's it! Have fun in foxverse!
